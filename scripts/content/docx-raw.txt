Nestled between rice fields, limestone mountains, and the peaceful Son River, Green Riverside Cosy Home is more than just a place to stay.

It's a place where travelers can experience the beauty of Phong Nha through the eyes of a local family.

My name is Linh, founder and host of Green Riverside. I was born and raised in the countryside of Phong Nha and always dreamed of building something meaningful for my family, my community, and the place I call home.

Before opening Green Riverside, I worked as an English teacher, tour guide, travel consultant, and receptionist. These experiences taught me the value of hospitality and inspired me to create a place where travelers could feel genuinely welcomed.

Today, Green Riverside is more than a hostel. It is a place to stay, eat, explore, and connect. Whether you're joining a family dinner, watching the sunset from our rooftop cafe, exploring the caves of Phong Nha, or simply relaxing by the river, we hope you'll feel at home here.

🌿 Phong Nha – The Kingdom of Caves

Phong Nha – Ke Bang National Park, located in Quang Tri Province in Central Vietnam, is known worldwide as the “Kingdom of Caves.” This UNESCO World Heritage Site was recognized in 2003 for its outstanding geological and geomorphological value, and again in 2015 for its exceptional biodiversity.

With more than 400 discovered caves, Phong Nha is one of the most spectacular cave systems on Earth. It is a destination where massive limestone mountains, underground rivers, jungle-covered valleys, and untouched wilderness come together to create a truly unique landscape that feels both ancient and alive.

Here, dramatic karst formations and vast cave systems have been shaped over millions of years, while peaceful rice fields, quiet villages, and the gentle flow of the Son River reveal a slower and more authentic side of rural Vietnam. From world-class jungle expeditions to easy sightseeing tours, Phong Nha offers something for every type of traveller.

This is not just a place to visit — it is a place to explore, discover, and experience nature in its purest form.

With over 400 caves and countless activities available across platforms like GetYourGuide, Tripadvisor, TikTok, Instagram, and other social media, it is very easy to feel overwhelmed and unsure about what to choose, how long to stay, or which experiences are truly worth it.

That is exactly where we can help.

Spend a Day Exploring the Stunning Bong Lai Valley (10–15 km from town)

Bong Lai Valley is one of the most unique countryside areas in Phong Nha, just 10–15 km from town. Known for its rice fields, rivers, farms, and fun local experiences, it is the perfect destination to discover authentic rural life beyond the caves.

While many travelers try to explore on their own by cycling or motorbike, the experience can sometimes feel confusing due to scattered attractions and hidden routes. This is why joining our Morning or Afternoon Cycling Tour to Bong Lai Valley & Cooking Class with Green Riverside Cosy Home is the easiest and most enjoyable way to experience the valley in a relaxed, meaningful, and time-efficient way.

With our local team, you don’t just visit places — you understand the stories, meet local people properly, and discover hidden spots that most travelers easily miss.

🚴 How to Explore Bong Lai Valley

You have three options to explore the valley:

🚲 Self cycling for independent travelers

🏍️ Motorbike ride for flexible exploration

👣 Morning or Afternoon Cycling Tour to Bong Lai Valley & Cooking Class with Green Riverside Cosy Home for a deeper, stress-free, and fully guided experience

Many guests choose the guided experience because it saves time, removes confusion on routes, and brings you directly to the best highlights in one smooth journey.

🐥 Duck Stop – The Most Iconic Fun Experience

Duck Stop is one of the most famous stops in Bong Lai Valley and a must-visit highlight.

Here you can become a “duck leader,” wear a traditional Vietnamese hat and funny shoes, and enjoy playful interaction with friendly ducks. The famous “duck foot massage” is one of the most memorable experiences in Phong Nha.

You can also enjoy a traditional Vietnamese pancake (Bánh Xèo) and a drink, making it both fun and delicious.

🐃 Duck Tang Farm – Real Local Farm Life

Duck Tang Farm offers an authentic countryside experience where you can truly connect with rural Vietnam.

Activities include buffalo riding, animal feeding, fishing, vegetable picking, and interacting with ducks, puppies, and turtles. You will also enjoy a freshly prepared organic meal made from local ingredients.

Everything is well arranged so you can simply relax and enjoy the experience without worrying about timing or logistics.

🌉 Monkey Bridge & Adventure Stops

Cuong Rung Farm offers fun and adventure with the famous Monkey Bridge, crazy swings, and countryside challenges.

It is perfect for laughter, light adrenaline, and memorable photo moments — and is naturally included in the route so you don’t miss the most exciting spots.

🍻 Pub with Cold Beer – Relax in Nature

Pub with Cold Beer is a great place to slow down during the journey.

Enjoy cold drinks, BBQ chicken, a swimming pool, and open valley views. It is the perfect afternoon break to relax and enjoy the peaceful countryside atmosphere.

🌊 Ồ Ồ Lake – Swim, Relax & Recharge

Ồ Ồ Lake is a beautiful natural swimming spot included in many recommended itineraries.

You can swim, enjoy paddle boarding, or simply relax by the water. Local food such as grilled chicken, pork with sticky rice, and fresh drinks are available, making it a perfect final stop of the day.

🌿 Funky Beach & East Hill – Hidden Sunset Gems

Funky Beach is a relaxed riverside spot where you can enjoy water activities, chill atmosphere, and local countryside food, especially roasted chicken and simple rural dishes.

Nearby, East Hill Phong Nha offers one of the most beautiful sunset viewpoints in the valley. This is often included in our route to end the day with golden light over rice fields and mountains.

2, A Perfect Alternative – Explore Phong Nha by Motorbike

If you prefer a different way to explore beyond the cycling tour to Bong Lai Valley, a motorbike adventure through Phong Nha – Ke Bang National Park is the perfect alternative.

While the cycling tour offers a slow, immersive countryside experience through rice fields, farms, and local villages, the motorbike routes open up a much wider journey — taking you deeper into dramatic limestone mountains, hidden valleys, winding back roads, and untouched natural landscapes.

It is the ideal option for travelers who want more freedom, flexibility, and a more adventurous way to experience Phong Nha at their own pace.

For a deeper overview of all available motorbike routes and detailed maps, you can also refer to this useful independent travel guide:👉 https://www.vietnamcoracle.com/phong-nha-by-motorbike-routes-loops/

Discover the Motorbike Loop Routes

With a motorbike, you can explore some of the most famous scenic loops in Phong Nha – Ke Bang National Park at your own pace, without needing to join a fixed group tour like the Ha Giang Loop.

Each route offers a different style of adventure depending on your time, experience, and interests:

🌿 Route 1: Caves & Country Loop (50–90 km)

A classic combination of countryside roads, local villages, and famous cave areas — perfect for a full-day scenic ride.

🌉 Route 2: Bridges & Back-Roads Loop (40 km)

A relaxed route through quiet rural roads, small bridges, and authentic local landscapes away from tourist crowds.

🌾 Route 3: Bong Lai Valley Loop (15 km)

A short and easy loop connecting farms, local experiences, and countryside highlights — ideal if you still want a taste of Bong Lai after cycling or as a standalone ride.

🏞️ Route 4: Ho Chi Minh West is Best Loop (290 km)

A long adventure along historic mountain roads, remote villages, and untouched natural scenery — ideal for experienced riders.

🐒 Route 5: King Kong Loop (190–220 km)

A legendary cinematic route through dramatic limestone mountains and famous filming locations of “King Kong: Skull Island.”

💚 Cycling Tour or Motorbike Adventure – Which One Should You Choose?

If you want a slow, social, and immersive countryside experience, our Morning or Afternoon Cycling Tour to Bong Lai Valley & Cooking Class with Green Riverside Cosy Home is the best choice.

But if you prefer freedom, flexibility, and covering more ground, then a motorbike adventure is the perfect alternative.

Many travelers choose to combine both experiences — cycling first for local life, then motorbike for deeper exploration of the national park.

🚨 Easy Rider & Private Car Option (Highly Recommended)

With a motorbike, you can explore freely — but if you are not confident riding on mountain or rural roads, you don’t need to miss the experience.

You can simply book our Easy Rider (local driver on motorbike) or private car tour, and enjoy the journey safely, comfortably, and stress-free.

Our Easy Riders are experienced local guides who know every hidden road, viewpoint, and local stop — giving you a much richer experience than going alone, without worrying about navigation or safety.

⚠️ Important: Ride-hailing services like Grab are very limited or unavailable in deeper areas of Phong Nha – Ke Bang National Park. Pre-arranged transport is strongly recommended, especially during high season.

4, Join an Adventure Cave Tour – A Must-Do in Phong Nha

Known as the "Kingdom of Caves" and the caving capital of Southeast Asia, Phong Nha is one of the most breathtaking wilderness areas in the region and home to some of the largest limestone formations in the world.

If you're an adventure lover, this is the place for you! One of the best ways to truly experience the magic of Phong Nha is by joining a small-group adventure cave tour—an unforgettable journey that combines stunning natural landscapes with thrilling outdoor activities.

Your adventure may include:

Trekking through ancient primeval jungle, rich with unique flora and fauna

Wading across cold, clear streams

Visiting remote ethnic minority villages hidden deep in the jungle

Rock climbing limestone karsts to reach hidden cave entrances

Exploring spectacular underground cave formations

Swimming and cliff-jumping into crystal-clear cave pools

Paddle boarding in complete underground silence

And if you're up for it, overnight camping inside the cave or jungle—a once-in-a-lifetime experience you’ll never forget

Beyond the adventure, you’ll be guided by experienced local tour leaders and safety assistants, enjoy delicious home-cooked meals prepared by local chefs, and be supported by porters who ensure everything runs smoothly.

This is more than just a tour—it’s a deep dive into one of the most extraordinary natural wonders on Earth. You’ll leave with memories of Phong Nha as the highlight of your travels.

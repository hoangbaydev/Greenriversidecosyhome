Explore

Tours & Adventures

Caves, kayaking, and local experiences — booked directly with us.

10–15 km from town · Authentic countryside

Bong Lai Valley

Bong Lai Valley is one of the most unique countryside areas near Phong Nha — rice fields, rivers, farms, and fun local experiences. Our Morning or Afternoon Cycling Tour & Cooking Class is the easiest, most meaningful way to explore without route confusion. You can self-cycle, ride a motorbike, or join our guided tour for local stories and hidden spots most independent travellers miss.

Bong Lai cycling tour

National park loop routes

Explore by Motorbike

A motorbike adventure opens up dramatic karsts, hidden valleys, and back roads at your own pace — the perfect alternative if you want more freedom than a cycling tour. Not confident riding? Book our Easy Rider or private car — ride-hailing is very limited in deeper areas of the national park, especially in high season.

Easy Rider & rentals

Easy · Relaxed · Must-see caves

National Classic Tours

Half-day and full-day tours to Phong Nha Cave, Paradise Cave, Dark Cave, and combinations — perfect if you have limited time and want iconic caves without complicated planning. 🎁 SPECIAL OFFER: FREE shuttle to Duck Tang Farm (Bong Lai Valley) for all Green Riverside guests on National Classic Tours. See our Tours page for full pricing from 650,000 VND.
